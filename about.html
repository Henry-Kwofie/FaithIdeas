About Us

We deal in poultry, piggery farming and pharmaceutical marketing and distribution.
Farming products sold are chicken and pork sausages, crates of eggs, local and foreign broilers and pigs.
The broilers and pigs are dressed on puchasing for our clients comfort.
We deal in wholesale and retailing of pharmaceutical and medical equipments.
We also have a pharmacy shop which provides medical consultancy with trained and licensed professionals, over the counter medications, first aid and medical check ups (blood pressure and glucose level check).
The company was founded by Mr. Stephen Kwofie in xxxx.
He graduated from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology in 2008 with Bsc Agriculture and further achieved a Masters of Philosophy in Logistics and Supply Chain Management in 2015.
FIC began as only a backyard poultry farm.
It has made giant strides since its commencement and has grown from a simple backyard poultry farm to a thriving company with sophisticated poultry and piggery farm, wholesale and retail shops for medical and pharmaceutical products, a community pharmacy shop and consultancy services in pharmaceuticals, health and supply chain management.
